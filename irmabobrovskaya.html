The idea

Irma Bobrovskaya is a unique project to work with. The goal of this client is to build a brand for selling services online. The idea was to make it look and feel elegant, simple, and modern at the same time. The brand simplicity was achieved through the pastel color palette. Modernity is presented through the animation. Elegance is shown in the font family matches.
